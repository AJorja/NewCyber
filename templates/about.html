Who We Are

Welcome to Happy Hedgehog Face Painting! We are a passionate team dedicated to spreading joy and creativity through the art of face painting. Our journey began with a simple idea: to bring smiles to faces, one stroke at a time. With years of experience and a love for what we do, we have become a trusted name in the industry, known for our exceptional service and commitment to quality. At Happy Hedgehog, we believe in the power of imagination and the magic of transformation. Each brushstroke is infused with care and attention to detail, turning every face into a canvas of wonder. Whether it's a birthday party, corporate event, or community gathering, we bring colour and excitement to every occasion, leaving lasting memories and happy faces behind.
